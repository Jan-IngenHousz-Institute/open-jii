# Glossary & Terminology

# Admin
An experiments'admin is capable of changing the settings of an experiment, including adding and removing other members.

### Experiment
Named container for one or more Flow- or protocol-driven Measurement sessions over time.  

### JII
Jan IngenHousz Institute

### FAIR Principles
Guidelines to make digital research assets Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, and Reusable.

### Macro
A reproducible data pipeline (aka ETL pipeline) step that transforms raw Bronze data into cleaned/curated Silver data for analysis (e.g., a Databricks job/notebook).  

### Measurement
A single time-stamped result set, plus metadata (who, where, device, protocol, macro, etc.). Often described as a “row” in the data model/csv.

### Member
A member of an experiment has less rights then an admin. A member can see and contribute measurement, but not change the settings of an experiment

### Protocol
Executable measurement recipe (steps, parameters, timings) the App/sensor follows to collect a Measurement.

### ORCID iD
Persistent researcher identifier used for login/attribution and contributor disambiguation. ORCID
