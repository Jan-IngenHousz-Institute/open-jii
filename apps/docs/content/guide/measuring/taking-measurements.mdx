The app runs the **pinned workbook version** attached to an experiment. That
workbook determines which instructions, questions, protocols, branches, and
analysis steps appear.

## Take a reading

1. Open the measurement flow and choose an experiment. The app labels flows as
   sensor required, questions only, or sensor plus questions.
2. If a sensor is required, open **Devices** and connect the MultispeQ.
3. Select **Start measurement** (or the named measurement action).
4. Follow the workbook in order. Answer required questions and act on any field
   instructions.
5. At a measurement step, select **Start measurement** and follow prompts from
   the instrument. Some protocols pause for clamp interaction.
6. Review the result and any macro output. Select **Accept data** to save it, or
   **Discard & retry** to measure again.
7. Check **Recent** for the saved reading and its sync state.

Workbook branches are evaluated on the device and may skip ahead or return to an
earlier step. Question-only flows finish without a MultispeQ reading.

## If something interrupts the flow

- **Pause** keeps the current answers and progress; **Discard** removes the
  attempt.
- If the device disconnects, reconnect it before retrying the measurement step.
- If upload is unavailable, the accepted reading remains local and can be synced
  later from **Recent**.
- For several wired devices, the result review is per device. See
  [Multi-device measurements](/guide/devices-protocols/multi-device).

The app records a synchronized timestamp and timezone. Although location access
may be requested, current code uses it to determine timezone rather than attach
GPS coordinates to the measurement.
