An **experiment** is the main organizing unit in openJII. It holds your
measurements, the people you work with, and the settings for a single study,
usually one plant species or one research question.

This section covers everything about setting up and running an experiment:

<Cards>
  <Card title="Creating an experiment" href="/guide/experiments/creating">
    Name your experiment, set who can see it, and get it ready for measurements.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Adding metadata" href="/guide/experiments/metadata">
    Attach context such as genotype, treatment, or planting date so your data is analyzable.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Managing & organizing experiments" href="/guide/experiments/managing">
    Find, search, and filter your experiments, and archive the ones you are done with.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Adding collaborators & roles" href="/guide/experiments/collaborators">
    Invite people to your experiment and understand what admins and members can do.
  </Card>
</Cards>

New to openJII? Start with the [quick start](/guide/get-started/quick-start) for
the fastest path to your first measurement.
