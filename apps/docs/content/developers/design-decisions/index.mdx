This section records current architecture decisions and preserves the original design records. Historical records explain what the team decided at the time; they are not automatically a description of the current implementation.

In particular, the accepted 2025 data ADRs describe per-experiment schemas and dual medallion pipelines. Those mechanisms are superseded by the current central `centrum` decision below.

## Current ADRs

- [Consolidate experiment data in centrum](/developers/design-decisions/centrum-data-architecture-adr) — replaces per-experiment schemas, pipelines, and provisioning with central experiment-keyed tables and views.
- [Replace ts-rest with oRPC](/developers/design-decisions/orpc-migration-adr) — current contract, runtime validation, client, OpenAPI, and REST-compatibility boundary.
- [Pin experiments to workbook snapshots](/developers/design-decisions/workbook-versioning-adr) — separates mutable authoring from immutable published execution, upgrade, and rollback.
- [Execute macros in isolated language sandboxes](/developers/design-decisions/macro-sandbox-adr) — current Python, JavaScript, and R execution and security boundary.
- [Use an offline-first mobile outbox](/developers/design-decisions/mobile-offline-outbox-adr) — current SQLite, retry, MQTT acknowledgement, and offline recovery boundary.
- [Replace Docusaurus with Fumadocs](/developers/design-decisions/fumadocs-migration-adr) — current decision for the documentation application, static hosting, routing, search, API generation, and cutover safety.

## Historical records and proposals

- [MQTT data-ingestion topic proposal](/developers/design-decisions/mqtt-topic-proposal) — introduced the versioned experiment ingestion topic that remains represented in `asyncapi.yaml`.
- [Data governance model ADR](/developers/design-decisions/data-governance-adr) — accepted in 2025; its per-experiment schemas and dual medallion model are superseded by the central `centrum` ADR.
- [Data ingestion architecture ADR](/developers/design-decisions/data-ingestion-adr) — accepted in 2025; its per-experiment pipelines and orchestration are superseded by the central `centrum` ADR.
